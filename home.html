How NetrOn Works

1. Data Collection

Sensors capture temperature, vibration, and voltage values.

2. Processing

Arduino filters and formats the sensor readings.

3. Transmission

Bluetooth module sends data to your mobile or web dashboard.

4. Monitoring

Dashboard displays live health metrics of your vehicle.

5. Alerts

Smart alerts notify you about overheating, faults, or voltage drops.

FAQs

Q: Is it suitable for all two-wheelers?

Yes, NetrOn is designed to be modular and fits most bikes and scooters.

Q: How is data transmitted?

Through a Bluetooth module connected to the onboard Arduino system.

Q: Does it require internet?

No internet needed for local monitoring. Only Bluetooth-enabled app is required.
